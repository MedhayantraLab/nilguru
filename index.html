About Us

Founded by Mata Debmaya Bhattarai, Nilkantha Babadham and Santoshi Maa Gurukul is a sacred temple and Sanskrit-based Gurukul school offering education from grades 5 to 12. We aim to preserve and promote our rich cultural and spiritual heritage through education and religious activities.

This non-profit charitable organization conducts various sacred rituals and worship, recently hosting a three-month-long Maha Yegya that attracted eminent personalities, including former kings, ministers, professors, doctors, and engineers.

Our Activities

We host regular religious ceremonies, spiritual discourses, and cultural events. Our Gurukul provides holistic education, combining academics with Sanskrit scriptures and Vedic traditions. Visit us to witness the harmony of spirituality and education.
